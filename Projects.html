HealthConnect

We designed HealthConnect with the aim of improving access to female intimate healthcare information for women in low-income urban areas of Pakistan. The system was designed leveraging the existing Lady Health Worker (LHW)  system to bridge the gap between women and the primary healthcare infrastructure in Pakistan.

MindStation

With MindStation, we worked towards a design solution targeting stigma surrounding mental healthcare. The solution was based on extensive primary research involving all stakeholders in our target usage location i.e. universities in Pakistan, specifically LUMS.

MobileMechanic

MobileMechanic was developed keeping in mind the gap between customers and mechanics. The interface was designed based on desk research, informal interviews, and anecdotal evidence. The app was modeled on the Uber system and developed using the MERN stack.
